A Flower Above The Clouds

This film is a 2019 Burmese romantic drama film, directed by Sin Yaw Mg Mg . The film was shot a joint venture between Myanmar and South Korea;produced by JBJ Entertainment and Big 5 Film Production premiered Myanmar on December 19, 2019.

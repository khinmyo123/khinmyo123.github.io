Endless Love


Endless Love is a 2014 Burmese thriller-drama film,
directed by Thein Han (Phoenix) starring Nay Toe,
Wai Lu Kyaw and Wutt Hmone Shwe Yi.
  • Nay Toe as Thurikza, Thura Satka (dual role)
  • Wai Lu Kyaw as Min Thiha, Thake Di (dual role)
  • Sai Thiha as Nanda
  • Wutt Hmone Shwe Yi as Twe Tar Oo, Yu Par (dual role)
  • Kyu Kyu Thin as Aunty Kyu
  • K Nyi as Phoe Lone
  • A Ni Zaw as Nan Phyu


Made in Heart

Made in Heart, is a 2014 Burmese drama film starring Pyay Ti Oo, Moe Hay Ko and Wutt Hmone Shwe Yi.The film, produced by Lucky Seven Film Production,premiered in Myanmar on October 3, 2014.
